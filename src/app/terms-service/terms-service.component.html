SERVICE DESCRIPTION

This Service provides owners of food and drink establishments that permit their patrons to pay for products at some future time, typically referred to as a "tab," to receive such payments electronically. The Service also allows patrons of participating establishments to make such payments without waiting for a service provider to take such payment manually. The Service is configured to charge a specific fee for such Services, and includes a gratuity. All such fees are disclosed tbbbout.com and are repeated here:

  • For users closing a tab with ACH/checking account - 3% fee is charged
  • For users closing a tab with Credit Card or other electronic payment (e.g. VENMO, PAYPAL, APPLE PAY, GOOGLE PAY, etc.) - 6% fee is charged.

We may "close" an open tab at any time that we determine that the products have been provided and the participating establishment has closed or the patron has left the establishment (but in any event, we may close all tabs within 24 hours after they are opened; typically at 0400). We may include advertising on our mobile application that is used by patrons, and we may use the email, mobile number and other service addresses of patrons to communicate with them with relevant advertising, such as advertising of establishments that use the Service.
